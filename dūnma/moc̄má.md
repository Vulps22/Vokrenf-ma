# Etymology of moc̄má

## IPA
- /mɒx.mja/

## English Translation
- right limb, right arm (right-extend)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: moc̄ + má)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- moc̄ (right) + má (extend/grow)

## Justification
- A rightward extension from the body. Used in body phrases: moc̄má c̄erem = right arm.
