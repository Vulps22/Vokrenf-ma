# Etymology of vīkret

## IPA
- /vɪk.rɛt/

## English Translation
- nose (smell-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: vīk + ret)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- vīk (smell) + ret (thing)

## Justification
- The thing through which one smells. Part of the sense organ series: vidret (eye), vekret (ear), vīkret (nose), vatret (mouth/tongue), vonret (skin).
