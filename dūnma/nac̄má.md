# Etymology of nac̄má

## IPA
- /nax.mja/

## English Translation
- lower limb, leg (down-extend)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: nac̄ + má)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- nac̄ (down) + má (extend/grow)

## Justification
- A downward extension from the body. Used in body phrases: nac̄má c̄erem = leg; retne nac̄má c̄erem = foot; ja nac̄má c̄erem = penis.
