# Etymology of keblok

## IPA
- /kɛb.lɔːk/

## English Translation
- link/hyperlink

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: keb + lok)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- keb (join) + lok (place)

## Justification
- A link connects (joins) one place to another, making keb (join) + lok (place) a natural fit.
