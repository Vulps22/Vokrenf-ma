# Etymology of rōknákif

## IPA
- /roʊk.nja.kiːf/

## English Translation
- telecommunications (instant transmission)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: rōk + nákif)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- rōk (send, transmit) + nákif (instant)

## Justification
- Telecommunications is the transmission of information instantaneously; rōk (transmit) contextualised by nákif (instant).
