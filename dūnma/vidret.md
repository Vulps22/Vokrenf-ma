# Etymology of vidret

## IPA
- /vid.rɛt/

## English Translation
- eye (see-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: vid + ret)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- vid (see) + ret (thing)

## Justification
- The thing through which one sees. Part of the sense organ series: vidret (eye), vekret (ear), vīkret (nose), vatret (mouth/tongue), vonret (skin).
