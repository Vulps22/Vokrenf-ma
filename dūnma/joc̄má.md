# Etymology of joc̄má

## IPA
- /dʒɒx.mjɑ/

## English Translation
- left limb, left arm (left-extend)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: joc̄ + má)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- joc̄ (left) + má (extend/grow)

## Justification
- A leftward extension from the body. Used in body phrases: joc̄má c̄erem = left arm.
