# Etymology of nákif

## IPA
- /nja.kiːf/

## English Translation
- instant (no duration)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: ná + kif)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- ná (negative) + kif (duration)

## Justification
- An instant is a moment with no appreciable duration; ná (negative) negates kif (duration).
