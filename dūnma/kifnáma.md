# Etymology of kifnáma

## English Translation
- short duration (duration-not-much)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: kif + náma)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- kif (duration) + náma (not a lot)

## Justification
- A small amount of duration; kif (duration) contextualised by náma (not a lot).
