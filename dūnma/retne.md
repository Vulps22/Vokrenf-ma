# Etymology of retne

## IPA
- /rɛt.nɛ/

## English Translation
- extremity, tip (end-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: ret + ne)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- ret (thing) + ne (end/tip)

## Justification
- Refers to the terminal point of any structure. Used in body phrases to identify the end of a limb (e.g. retne jīc̄má c̄erem = head; retne nac̄má c̄erem = foot).
