# Etymology of vokrettan

## English Translation
- story (spoken thing through time)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: vok + ret + tan)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- vok (speak) + ret (thing) + tan (time)

## Justification
- A story is a spoken thing that unfolds through time; vok (speak) + ret (thing) contextualised by tan (time).
