# Etymology of náma

## IPA
- /nja.ma/

## English Translation
- small, short, not a lot (not-much)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: ná + ma)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- ná (negative) + ma (lot, quantity)

## Justification
- The negation of ma (lot/quantity) yields its opposite: a small or limited amount.
