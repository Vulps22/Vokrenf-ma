# Etymology of c̄erem

## IPA
- /xɛr.ɛm/

## English Translation
- torso, body (core-person)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: c̄er + em)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- c̄er (core) + em (person)

## Justification
- The body is the core of a person. Used as the base noun in all body part constructions.
