# Etymology of kletrōknákif

## English Translation
- livestream (passively broadcast instantly)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: klet + rōk + nákif)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- klet (passive) + rōk (broadcast) + nákif (instant)

## Justification
- A livestream is something that is passively received as it is broadcast in real time; klet (passive) applied to rōk (broadcast) contextualised by nákif (instant).
