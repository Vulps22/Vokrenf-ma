# Etymology of vikret

## IPA
- /viːk.rɛt/

## English Translation
- nose (smell-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: vik + ret)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- vik (smell) + ret (thing)

## Justification
- The thing through which one smells. Part of the sense organ series: vidret (eye), vekret (ear), vikret (nose), vatret (mouth/tongue), vonret (skin).
