# Etymology of vatret

## IPA
- /vat.rɛt/

## English Translation
- mouth, tongue (taste-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: vat + ret)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- vat (taste) + ret (thing)

## Justification
- The thing through which one tastes. Part of the sense organ series: vidret (eye), vekret (ear), vīkret (nose), vatret (mouth/tongue), vonret (skin).
