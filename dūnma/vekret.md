# Etymology of vekret

## IPA
- /vɛk.rɛt/

## English Translation
- ear (hear-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: vek + ret)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- vek (hear) + ret (thing)

## Justification
- The thing through which one hears. Part of the sense organ series: vidret (eye), vekret (ear), vīkret (nose), vatret (mouth/tongue), vonret (skin).
