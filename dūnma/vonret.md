# Etymology of vonret

## IPA
- /vɒn.rɛt/

## English Translation
- skin (touch-thing)

## Word Type
- dūnma (multi-syllable: von + ret)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- von (touch) + ret (thing)

## Justification
- The thing through which one touches. Part of the sense organ series: vidret (eye), vekret (ear), vīkret (nose), vatret (mouth/tongue), vonret (skin).
