# Etymology of ja

## IPA
- /dʒa/

## English Translation
- Positive polarity/preference marker; front (spatial)

## Word Type
- dūn (single syllable)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- N/A

## Justification
- The forward/positive polarity of ja extends naturally to spatial meaning: forward = front. No new dūn is needed for "front"; ja serves this role when used in spatial context (e.g. ja nac̄má c̄erem = front of lower limb = penis; ja jīc̄má c̄erem = front of upper limb = breast).
