# Etymology of klīv

## English Translation
- Reflexive voice marker

## Word Type
- dūn (single syllable)

## Breakdown (for dūnma only)
- N/A

## Justification
- Part of the kl- valency operator set. Reflexive voice indicates the subject acts upon itself (agent = patient). Uses ī /ɪ/ — a vowel introduced with this word — chosen for its compact, inward quality, fitting for an action that turns back on the subject.
